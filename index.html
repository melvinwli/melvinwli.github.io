About Snow Path Immigration Consultancy

Snow Path Immigration Consultancy is an immigration service provider based in Toronto, dedicated to helping individuals and families navigate the complex immigration process with ease.

Melvin Li is the founder of Snow Path Immigration Consultancy and is a licensed and registered immigration and citizenship consultant who graduated from the Faculty of Law at Queen’s University with a Graduate Diploma in Immigration and Citizenship Law. He provides impartial immigration and citizenship consultancy to individuals who are looking to settle in Canada permanently. In addition to his degree in Hospitality Management from the Suriname College of Hospitality and Tourism Management, Melvin also holds a Master's degree in Tourism Management from Beijing International Studies University. He has been living in Canada for many years and has a strong passion for supporting newcomers. Melvin takes a keen interest in listening to the stories of newcomers and temporary workers, whether it is through his work or his support of religious institutions that aid in their transition. Unfortunately, Melvin has also been a victim of fraudulent consultants, which is why he is committed to providing honest and reliable services to all of his clients.

At Snow Path Immigration Consultancy, we believe in transparency, integrity, and excellence in everything we do. Our mission is to empower our clients to achieve their immigration goals and start a new chapter in their lives.
